Privacy

This site runs cookieless, self-hosted analytics. No third party ever sees your visit, and nothing you read here follows you anywhere else.

We run our own instance of Umami, an open-source analytics tool, to see which pages get read and where visitors come from. It sets no cookies and stores no cross-site identifier: there is nothing here to opt out of because there is nothing that tracks you between sites or across visits.

What it records per page view: the page, the referrer, and coarse browser/OS/device and country information. Your IP address is never stored — Umami hashes it together with your browser and this site's ID, using a salt it rotates every day, purely to distinguish a return visit from a new one within that day. The raw address never touches disk.

This applies to Obsign and Custos, the two pages of this marketing site — and nowhere else. It is never present in the Obsign or Admin Companion apps, on the Custos server itself, or on any page that touches your account, your identity, or your keys.

The dashboard lives on infrastructure we operate; the data never leaves it, and it is never sold or shared. The source for this site is public, same as everything else we run.
